Good to know

Before you
book.

How long should I block off?

Cuts run about an hour and color takes two to four. Corrections, balayage, and bridal trials can stretch across a whole afternoon, but Abi will give you an honest estimate when you book.

Do you take walk-ins?

Abi is appointment only, but booking on Square only takes a minute.

Where exactly are you?

Inside Bella Bouture Beauty Studio at 109 W Minnesota Park Rd, Suite 5, in Hammond. Park right in front of the building and come on in.

What's the cancellation policy?

There's no cancellation fee. If something comes up, just let her know as early as you can so the spot can go to somebody else.

Do you require a deposit?

Booking happens on Square. If your service takes a deposit, you'll see the amount before you confirm anything.

How should I show up to a color appointment?

Come with day-old hair and go easy on the product. Pictures help a lot too, even blurry ones from your camera roll.

Do you work on textured and curly hair?

Yes. Waves, curls, and coils are all welcome for cuts, color, and shaping. Come in with a fresh wash-and-go so she can see how your hair naturally sits.

Where do I park?

There's parking right in front of the building, a few steps from the door.
